Music is an important way we can worship and reflect all throughout the year, but especially during the Advent and Christmas season.

Most of us hear the song “O Come, O Come, Emmanuel” many times each December, but have you ever taken time to stop and truly reflect on its lyrics? We encourage you to spend a few minutes today listening to its rich refrains and celebrating Christ’s coming. If you would like Scripture accompaniment, read Isaiah 60 and Isaiah 7:14.
