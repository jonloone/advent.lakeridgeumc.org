Today’s Advent activity involves a challenge for you and your family! The Christmas season is a unique opportunity for us to share the love of Christ and the hope that we have in His coming to earth. It is also a time when our church will welcome in many visitors who do not otherwise attend services. We encourage you to join us in inviting a neighbor or friend to one of Lakeridge’s Christmas Eve services! We hope that these services will be a blessing not only to you and your family, but also to those in Lubbock who are not yet part of a church community.
