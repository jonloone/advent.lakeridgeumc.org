This week’s excerpt from J.D. Walt’s book “Reset: Advent Devotions for the Whole Family” is all about resetting our priorities so that God is first in our lives. This can be easy to forget during the busy days leading up to Christmas, so we invite you to read this devotional and join us in redirecting our gaze towards the true reason for the season.
