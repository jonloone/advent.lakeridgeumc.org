In a season of holiday haste and hustle, Advent causes us to slow down and prepare. In a world of darkness and violence, the Church proclaims a message of light and hope. In a world of division and fear, the coming of the Christ child reminds us of the healing power of love.

Whether or not you or your family have celebrated the Advent season before, we encourage you to take a few minutes of your day to read more about it and the reasons why we might strive to incorporate it into our daily routines this December.

We also encourage you and your family to join us at one of our services tomorrow as we reflect and worship together as a community.
